The Terroir

Jwaya's Microclimate

Our fields sit at 33.2°N latitude on a limestone plateau. The unique combination of calcium-rich soil and arid summers forces the Aloe plant to store excess nutrients, resulting in a polysaccharide count 3x higher than industry standards.

Chemical-Free Cultivation

We do not use synthetic pesticides, herbicides, or fertilizers. Pure nature, strictly controlled.

Smart Drip Irrigation

Sensors trigger watering only when needed, using harvested rainwater powered by solar energy.

Greenhouse Protected

Plants are shielded from direct harsh UV radiation to preserve leaf succulence and prevent oxidation.

Hand Filleted

Harvested at dawn. Filleted by hand within 2 hours to ensure maximum nutrient retention.
